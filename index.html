Your Host

Kevine Nokam

Colorado Realtor · Community Educator · First-Generation Immigrant

Kevine Nokam is a Colorado Realtor dedicated to helping immigrants, Afro-descendants, and first-generation families turn homeownership into a pathway to stability and generational wealth.

Originally from Cameroon, Kevine understands the immigrant journey personally. She knows what it feels like to start over in a new country, navigate unfamiliar systems, and work hard to create opportunities for your family. That experience is what drives her passion for real estate and community education today.

With over 10 years of experience in accounting and finance, Kevine combines strong analytical skills with a people-first approach to help clients confidently navigate the buying, selling, and investing process. She specializes in first-time homebuyers, new construction, relocation clients, and families looking to build wealth through real estate in the Denver Metro area.

As a wife, mother of four, investor, and first-generation immigrant herself — Kevine believes real estate is more than buying a house. It is about creating security, opportunity, and a legacy for future generations.
